5 Project Proposal - River Witham Ecological Enhancement Works Tender

5.1 Introduction

We are pleased to submit our proposal for the River Witham Ecological Enhancement Works Tender. Our company is eager to take part in this important project and contribute to the ecological improvement of the River Witham in Grantham.

5.2 Company Overview

5.2.1 Background

Oreooo is a reputable environmental services provider with a proven track record of successfully delivering similar projects. We are committed to sustainable practices and environmental conservation.

5.2.2 Expertise

Our team consists of highly skilled professionals with expertise in habitat restoration, river channel improvements, and ecological enhancements. We have successfully completed projects that align with the objectives outlined in the tender.

5.3 Proposed Approach

5.3.1 Lot 1: River Witham Ecological Enhancement

Objective: To enhance the ecological value along the River Witham at Stonebridge Close and Sedgwick Meadows, Grantham.

Proposed Works:

  • Creation of berms

  • Installation of coir rolls

  • Installation of woody deflectors

  • Introduction of gravel

  • Berm repair and planting

  • Volunteer engagement

5.3.2 Lot 2: Environmental Improvements and Increased Accessibility

Objective: To enhance the ecological value along the River Witham through floodplain reconnection at Belton Parkland, Grantham.

Proposed Works:

  • Tree hinging

  • Bank lowering

  • Installation of in-channel woody materials

  • Placement of floodplain woody materials

  • Creation of beaver dam analogues using posts and faggots

  • Scrape creation

5.10 Further Study

Out of my interests, I read the essay provided by lecturer, Economic Assessment of the Urban Heat Island Effect, and try to figure out the issues raised by UHI.AECON,2012

Firstly, the heat island effect has a significant impact on the urban environment, especially the increase in temperature, which not only affects the health and quality of life of urban residents, but also increases energy consumption and related economic costs. From a government perspective, taking steps to mitigate the heat island effect would not only help combat climate change, but could also lead to economic savings, such as energy savings through reduced air conditioning use and lower healthcare costs through improved public health. AECON,2012 Through this article, we have seen more effects of heat island effect on the economy. By combining policy and economic factors, I can reflect and study heat island effect in a more comprehensive way.

I was pleasantly surprised to find that the government’s approach to global warming from the heat island effect is a win-all strategy. It can not only improve the urban environment and improve the quality of life of residents, but also bring economic savings and benefits, especially in reducing energy consumption, improving public health and promoting green urban planning. Before I came into contact with the direction of environmental protection, I was always confused about the solution to the problem of global warming, because I felt that it was a very general plan, and the government did not know what kind of city-wide measures to improve the greenhouse effect. Therefore, after learning the term UHI, I have more ideas for policy makers and urban planners to incorporate measures to mitigate the heat island effect into urban development and climate change adaptation strategies, start from the comfort of people’s lives, and work up and down to solve the greenhouse effect in a more efficient way.
